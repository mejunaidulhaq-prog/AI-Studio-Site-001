# Custom Background Music Instructions

To play your own favorite song as the background music:

1. Drop your custom audio file into this `public/` folder.
2. Rename the file to `song.mp3`.
3. The app is already pre-programmed to automatically scan for `/song.mp3` first, and will seamlessly switch to it instead of the default streaming serene piano track.

Enjoy your custom digital sanctuary soundtrack! 🌸🕊
